Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

Comforting and timeless, perfect for any day.

Servings: 6
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, peeled & sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 8 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded (rotisserie works great)
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Sauté the veggies: In a large pot over medium heat, warm the olive oil. Add onion, carrots, and celery; cook until softened, about 5–7 minutes. Stir in garlic and cook 1 more minute.
  2. Build the broth: Pour in the chicken broth, then add shredded chicken, thyme, and the bay leaf. Bring to a gentle boil.
  3. Cook the noodles: Once boiling, add egg noodles and simmer until tender, about 8–10 minutes.
  4. Season & serve: Remove bay leaf, taste and season with salt and pepper. Ladle into bowls and sprinkle with fresh parsley.

Tip: For extra depth, sauté the veggies in a pat of butter alongside the oil—or use homemade bone broth if you have it!
